Jeonghun Cho

Jeonghun Cho was a senior engineer at Hynix Semiconductor from 2003 to 2005. Main role was development of a C compiler for 8-bit microcontrollers. He is currently a professor in the School of EE of Kyungpook National University, Daegu, South Korea since 2005. His research interest includes a binary translation, software safety and security for automotive, AUTOSAR, run-time monitoring and logging for embedded system. Dr. Cho is a member of IEEE.
