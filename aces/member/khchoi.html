Kiho Choi

KIHO CHOI (M.S. Candidate) received the B.S. degree in electronics engineering from Kyungpook National University, Daegu, Korea in 2017. He is currently a M.S student in department of electronics engineering at Kyungpook National university, Daegu, Korea. His research interests include Memory-embedded microprocessor architecture and its optimizing control algorithm.
